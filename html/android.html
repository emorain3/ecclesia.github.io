On The Spectrum

is an android app that gives voice to those without in times of crisis. We do this by enabling text-to-voice over a call and prioiritizing simple navigation, accesibility personalization, and providing pre-scripted messages options that succinctly describe the user's disability as a nonverbal individual.

Our client employed our help to develop an Android application that would enable nonverbal individuals, particularly autistic nonverbal individuals, to communicate over a call placed to 911 when in an emergency situation. Before a call is placed the user can input their message, then place the call and have it delivered as a voice message to the 911 dispatcher. I was responsible for developing the essential feature of the app's text-to-speech via phone network funcitonality. Additionally, I was responsible for corresponding with our city's officials to ensure that our application followed all regulations when contacting a federal public service and responsible for developing the system architecture and component design documentation for our Design Document linked here .
